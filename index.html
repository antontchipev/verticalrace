Vertical Race

Annual Ski Mountaineering Competition

Organized by the Luxembourgish Ski Federation (fls.lu) and the Lux Mountaineering Organization (flera.lu).

About the Competition

The Vertical Race is an annual amateur ski mountaineering competition that challenges participants with a thrilling ascent over scenic mountainous terrain. Every year, competitors from across the region gather to test their skills and endurance in a high-altitude race to the top!
